# PROMPT DALL-E - Axe 4 ZenClass

## Prompt principal (copiez ceci):

```
Flat design 2.0 illustration of a warm, safe classroom scene for ZenClass platform. 5-7 stylized student silhouettes in soft pastel colors (sky blue #87CEEB, sage green #9DC183, solar yellow #F4E4A6, terracotta #C4A484, cloud white #F8F9FA) stand in a circle holding hands or shoulders, forming a "human sun" symbolizing emotional warmth. In the center of the circle, a stylized heart pulses gently with luminous particles floating around. Background shows a stylized classroom with large windows letting in golden morning light. Green plants in corners symbolizing growth and life. Above the group, a speech bubble with "Ça va ? Je t'aide !" in friendly rounded text. In the top right corner, the ZenClass logo in a semi-transparent sky blue rounded frame floating like a benevolent guardian. Small stars and luminous particles scattered around students symbolizing small moments of well-being. Very soft pastel tones, rounded lines, feeling of security and shared warmth. Clean vector style with soft shadows.
```
